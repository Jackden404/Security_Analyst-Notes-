## 🏗️ Cloud Architectures & Responsibility Splits

Organizations must understand the boundaries of virtual asset ownership.

### IaaS Guest OS Management

- In the Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) model, the guest operating system patching is the sole responsibility of: **The customer**.

### The Shared Responsibility Model

[!IMPORTANT] **Defensive Boundaries:** The Shared Responsibility Model explicitly divides: **Security responsibilities between customer and provider**. The provider manages the underlying cloud infrastructure (Security OF the Cloud), while the customer manages identities, rules, and data classification (Security IN the Cloud).
