## Measuring Effectiveness

Track: hunts conducted, hypotheses tested, true positives found, new detections created, MTTD improvement.

## Coverage Metrics

Measure: % of MITRE ATT&CK techniques covered, % of critical assets monitored, gap analysis.

## ROI of Threat Hunting

Quantify: threats found before damage, cost of incidents prevented, time saved through automation.

## Reporting to Leadership

Communicate in business terms: risks identified, improvements made, resource requirements, industry benchmarks.
