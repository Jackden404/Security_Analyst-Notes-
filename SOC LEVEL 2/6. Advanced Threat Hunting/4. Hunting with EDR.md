## Process-Based Hunting

Search for suspicious patterns: processes from temp directories, unusual parent-child relationships, encoded command lines, high resource usage.

## File-Based Hunting

Look for: recently created executables, double extensions, high-entropy files, unexpected modifications.
## Registry-Based Hunting

Search for persistence: new Run keys, service modifications, WMI subscriptions, AppInit_DLLs changes.

## Network-Based Hunting

Detect: connections to newly registered domains, unusual DNS patterns, large data transfers, encrypted traffic to unknown destinations.
