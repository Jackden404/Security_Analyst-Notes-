## Hunting Maturity Model

Level 0: Ad-hoc. 
Level 1: Basic IOC searching. 
Level 2: Documented hypotheses. 
Level 3: Dedicated team. 
Level 4: Metrics-driven. 
Level 5: ML-assisted.

## Intelligence-Driven Hunting

Start with threat intelligence: What APT groups target your industry? What TTPs do they use? Build hypotheses from intelligence and hunt for evidence.

## Data-Driven Hunting

Analyze your own data for anomalies: baseline normal behavior, look for deviations, use statistical methods, focus on high-value assets.

## Hypothesis Development

Good hypotheses are specific and testable. Bad: Something might be wrong. Good: An attacker may be using PowerShell to download payloads from external domains.

## The Hunting Loop

1. Form hypothesis 
2. Identify data sources 
3. Develop queries 
4. Execute hunt 
5. Analyze results 
6. Document findings 
7. Create detections
