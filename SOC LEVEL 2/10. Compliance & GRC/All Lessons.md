# 1. GRC Fundamentals
## What is GRC?

Governance: policies and oversight. 
Risk: identifying and managing threats. 
Compliance: meeting regulatory requirements.

## Risk Management Frameworks

NIST RMF: 7-step process. 
ISO 31000: International standard. 
COSO: Internal control framework.

## NIST Cybersecurity Framework

Five functions: 
Identify (assets, risk), 
Protect (access, training), 
Detect (monitoring), 
Respond (planning, 
communications), 
Recover (improvements).

## ISO 27001

Information Security Management System. Risk-based approach. Continuous improvement. Requires regular audits.

# 2. SOC 2 Compliance

## Trust Service Criteria

1. Security: Protection against unauthorized access. 
2. Availability: System uptime. 
3. Processing Integrity: Accurate processing. 
4. Confidentiality: Protected information. 
5. Privacy: Personal data handling.
## Evidence Collection

Collect: access controls, change management, monitoring logs, incident procedures, risk assessments.
## Audit Preparation

Start 3-6 months before: gap analysis, policy review, control testing, evidence organization, remediation.
## Common Findings

Missing policies, inadequate access controls, insufficient logging, lack of IR procedures, missing risk assessments.

---
# 3. GDPR and Data Privacy
## Key Principles

Lawfulness, purpose limitation, data minimization, accuracy, storage limitation, integrity/confidentiality, accountability.
## Data Subject Rights

Access, rectification, erasure, portability, object to processing.
## Breach Notification

72 hours to supervisory authority. High risk: notify individuals. Document all breaches.
## Privacy Impact Assessments

Required for high-risk processing: describe activities, assess necessity, identify risks, implement mitigation.

# 4. Audit Preparation
## Audit Types

Internal: self-assessment. 
External: third-party. 
Compliance: against regulations. 
Surprise: unannounced.
## Evidence Automation

Script-based collection, automated compliance checks, continuous monitoring, centralized repository.
## Finding Remediation

Classify severity, assign ownership/deadlines, implement corrective actions, document, verify.
## Continuous Compliance

Automated monitoring, real-time policy enforcement, regular assessments, trend tracking.

---
# 5.Risk Assessment

## Risk Identification

Identify: assets, threats, vulnerabilities, controls.
## Risk Analysis

Qualitative: High/Medium/Low. Quantitative: ALE = SLE x ARO.
## Risk Treatment

Mitigate (reduce), Transfer (insure), Accept (monitor), Avoid (eliminate).
## Risk Monitoring

Track risks, monitor controls, update register, report to leadership.
